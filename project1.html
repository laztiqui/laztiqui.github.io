Laser Communication Testbed

NASA is currently developing laser communication systems to enable higher communication speeds across space. This system will support the Artemis lunar missions. As an NMSU capstone team, our job is to design a testbed to simulate the lunar environment and develop code for several laser communication modulation schemes. The testbed has a special type of dust that simulates lunar regolith. The testbed stirs up the dust, simulating a rocket landing on the surface of the moon. We will shine the laser through the testbed to measure how well each modulation scheme performs in this simulated environment.
